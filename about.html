About Me

Michigan Native

I was first introduced to photography early in college. An old manual Minolta camera, and a love of the dark room were fond memories.

I enjoy all avenues of photography including portraiture, sports and action, weddings, and real estate. A great photo can achieve so much in today's world. It tells a story. That's what I love so much about the craft.

I have worked for a well known, national photography company for almost 10 years now. I currently focus on sports and high school senior portraits with that company as their lead photographer. I also contract other photography events on my own time.

Fun Fact: I have officiated ice hockey at many levels for 25 years. I love the sport and grew up with it in Michigan, as well as other states.
